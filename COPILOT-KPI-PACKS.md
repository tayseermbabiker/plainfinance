# **1. PRODUCT / RETAIL (Physical Stores)**  
Retail is margin‑sensitive, inventory‑heavy, and foot‑traffic dependent.

## 🎯 Core KPIs
- **Gross Margin %**  
- **Inventory Days (DIO)**  
- **Revenue per Square Foot**  
- **Shrinkage %**  
- **Cash vs AP Ratio**  
- **Average Transaction Value (ATV)**  
- **Units per Transaction (UPT)**  
- **Return Rate %**  
- **Stockout Rate**  

## 🧮 Formulas
- Gross Margin = (Revenue – COGS) / Revenue  
- DIO = (Inventory / COGS) × 30  
- Shrinkage = (Expected Inventory – Actual) / Expected  
- ATV = Revenue / Number of Transactions  

## 📊 Benchmarks
| KPI | Healthy | Warning | Danger |
|-----|---------|---------|--------|
| Gross Margin | 40–55% | 25–40% | <25% |
| DIO | 30–60 days | 60–90 | >90 |
| Shrinkage | <1% | 1–2% | >2% |
| Cash vs AP | >1.5x | 1–1.5x | <1x |

## 🚨 Insight Triggers
- DIO > 90 → “You have too much cash trapped in slow‑moving stock.”  
- Shrinkage > 2% → “Inventory loss is eating margin.”  

## 🔧 Action Triggers
- Identify top 5 slow movers  
- Reduce SKU count  
- Negotiate vendor terms
