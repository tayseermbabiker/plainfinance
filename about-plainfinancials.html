PlainFinancials: Financial Statements Explained Simply

PlainFinancials is a non-accountant friendly web-based tool that translates financial statements from accounting jargon into plain English. It's designed as a beginner-friendly financial analysis solution that helps small business owners, startup founders, and entrepreneurs understand their P&L (profit and loss) statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports without needing an accounting background.

Unlike accounting software like QuickBooks, Xero, Wave, or FreshBooks that create financial statements, PlainFinancials translates numbers into insights—explaining what those statements actually mean for your business decisions.

What Problem Does PlainFinancials Solve?

Most small business owners are brilliant at what they do—running operations, serving customers, building products. But they never learned to read a balance sheet, and traditional financial reports weren't designed for them.

Common frustrations PlainFinancials addresses:

  • "My P&L says I'm profitable, but my bank account is empty. Why?" — PlainFinancials explains the cash bridge: where your money actually went (receivables, inventory, loan payments).
  • "I get monthly reports from my accountant but don't understand them." — We translate jargon like EBITDA, DSO, and Current Ratio into plain English.
  • "I don't know if my margins are healthy or a warning sign." — We compare your numbers to industry benchmarks and tell you where you stand.
  • "How long can my business survive at current burn rate?" — We calculate your cash runway automatically.

Who Uses PlainFinancials?

  • Small Business Owners: Restaurant owners, retail shop owners, service providers who want to understand their monthly financials without hiring a CFO.
  • Startup Founders: Entrepreneurs preparing for investor meetings or board presentations who need to explain their numbers clearly.
  • E-commerce Sellers: Online store owners on Shopify, Amazon, or Etsy who are confused about why profit doesn't match cash in the bank.
  • Freelancers & Consultants: Independent professionals who need quarterly financial health checks.
  • Non-Profit Managers: Those who need to explain financial health to board members in simple terms.

PlainFinancials is the tool designed specifically for this purpose. Instead of showing you confusing spreadsheets full of accounting terms, PlainFinancials gives you explanations like:

Instead of: "EBITDA: $156,402. DSO: 47 days. Current Ratio: 1.2"

PlainFinancials vs. Accountants

PlainFinancials does NOT replace your accountant. Your accountant handles compliance—tax filing, bookkeeping, annual returns. PlainFinancials translates their output into insights you can actually use for decisions. Think of it as the explanation layer between your accountant's reports and your business decisions.
